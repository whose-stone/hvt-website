We make sure the automation layer is useful, observable, and sustainable.

PLATFORMS & EXAMPLES

Tools we deploy in this service area

Each platform comes with a different delivery pattern, but the goal is the same: faster value, clearer governance, and stronger operations.

PLATFORM

Make

HVT uses Make to automate multi-step workflows across marketing, operations, CRM, and internal tooling with clear branching and alerting.

Example: Automating lead qualification and handoff from web forms into CRM, Slack alerts, enrichment tools, and follow-up task creation.

PLATFORM

n8n

We configure n8n where organizations want open, extensible automation that can bridge SaaS tools, APIs, and custom logic.

Example: Building an n8n workflow that syncs product events into a support workflow, updates internal systems, and escalates incidents when thresholds are breached.

PLATFORM

Zapier

HVT helps teams standardize Zapier usage for common workflows while introducing governance so quick wins do not become long-term sprawl.

Example: Launching approved Zapier automations for customer onboarding emails, CRM updates, and shared status reporting across go-to-market teams.

PLATFORM

Power Apps

For Microsoft-centric organizations, we support Power Apps solutions that streamline approvals, field data capture, and operational task management.

Example: Deploying a Power Apps intake application tied to SharePoint, Teams notifications, and Power Automate approval chains for finance operations.
