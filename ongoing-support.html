PLATFORMS & EXAMPLES

Tools we deploy in this service area

Each platform comes with a different delivery pattern, but the goal is the same: faster value, clearer governance, and stronger operations.

PLATFORM

SLA

HVT can operate against agreed response and resolution expectations so platform owners know how incidents and requests will be handled.

Example: Establishing a support retainer with severity tiers, response targets, weekly triage, and monthly service reporting for a distributed analytics estate.

PLATFORM

Monitoring

We set up and review monitoring signals across data refreshes, cloud services, dashboard failures, automation errors, and AI workflow quality indicators.

Example: Implementing alerts for failed ingestion jobs, stale dashboards, API threshold breaches, and unusual support ticket volumes tied to a data release.

PLATFORM

Training

HVT delivers ongoing onboarding, refresher sessions, role-based walkthroughs, and office hours so internal users stay effective even as tools change.

Example: Running quarterly training for analysts and business users after a Power BI and Snowflake rollout, including dashboard standards and troubleshooting guides.

PLATFORM

Audits

We conduct periodic audits to surface technical debt, adoption issues, access problems, and commercial inefficiencies before they become systemic risks.

Example: Completing a quarterly stack health review covering warehouse cost, dashboard adoption, low-code automation ownership, and AI governance compliance.
